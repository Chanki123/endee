Ohm’s Law states that the current through a conductor is directly proportional
to the voltage across it and inversely proportional to resistance.

Kirchhoff’s Voltage Law states that the sum of all voltages in a closed loop
is equal to zero.

The OSI model consists of seven layers: Physical, Data Link, Network,
Transport, Session, Presentation, and Application.

TCP is a connection-oriented protocol that provides reliable data transfer.

Python is an interpreted programming language widely used in data science
and machine learning.
